  1. Download your stats card using the button above. It will save to your camera roll.
  2. Open Instagram and tap the + button to create a new Story.
  3. Choose a photo or video from your camera roll as your background, or use a solid colour.
  4. Tap the sticker icon at the top of the screen, then tap Photo.
  5. Select your stats card from your camera roll. It will appear as a sticker you can resize and reposition.
  6. Tag @silentbookclub and share!

  2. Open Instagram and create a new Reel.
  3. Record or upload your video as the base layer.
  4. Tap the sticker icon, then Photo, and select your stats card from your camera roll.
  5. Resize and position the card anywhere on screen. Pinch to scale it down for a corner overlay look.
  6. Add music, tag @silentbookclub, and post!
  1. Download your stats card using the button above.
  2. Open CapCut and start a new project with your video.
  3. Tap Overlay at the bottom, then Add Overlay.
  4. Select your stats card from your camera roll.
  5. Resize, reposition and set the duration to match your clip. You can also adjust opacity for a subtle look.
  6. Tip: Use CapCut's Freeze Frame feature to hold a still moment — great for letting your stats linger on screen.
  7. Export your video and share it, tagging @silentbookclub!
